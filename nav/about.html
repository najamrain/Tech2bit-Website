About Us


We Are Team Watch Us Build An Empire Together

TECH2BIT has everything you need

Established in 2015, TECH2BIT is comprised of an expert team of solution architects, system engineers and project managers. We are dedicated to supporting the IT needs of the small and medium-sized business sector in the Dayton, Cincinnati and Columbus areas.

Just as every client is different, every solution is different, too. We take the time to gain an understanding of your unique IT challenges. Then we base our solution on how much value it adds to your business, including:

  • Fully managed IT services
  • Network standardization and comprehensive documentation
  • Quality service delivery in a single model with no additional fees
TECH2BIT provides a smart business solution to manage your IT network.
