Reliable

communications

Reliable group calling: the common mode of operation of TETRA is in a group calling mode in which a single button push on the hand-held terminal will connect the user to the users in a selected call group and/or a dispatcher. It is also possible for the terminal to act as a one-to-one ‘walkie talkie’ but without the normal range limitation since the call still uses the network.

Flexibility and versatility: TETRA hand-held terminals can also be used as mobile phones (cell phones), with a direct connection to other TETRA users or to the public phone system. Emergency buttons, provided on the terminals, also enable the users to transmit emergency signals, to the dispatcher, overriding any other activity taking place at the same time.
